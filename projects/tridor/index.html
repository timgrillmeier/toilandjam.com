This is Tridor

Tridor is a dynamic, HTML5 canvas animation. The initial tone and the rate of tone-change for each triangle is randomly generated when the page is first loaded. This results in each triangle having a unique shade and transition rate.

Currently, Tridor is monochromatic but will be expanded in the future to support many colours.
