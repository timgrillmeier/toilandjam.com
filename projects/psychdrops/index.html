Psychdrops

Psychdrops is a dynamic, HTML5 canvas animation. Random x, y coordinates are generated and added to an array of active drops. These drops grow out over the canvas over time. If you move your cursor over the web page, the randomly generated drops will start generating at the position of your cursor. Try drawing a figure-8 over the page with your cursor. After a short period of inactivity from your cursor, the drops will start generating at random locations once again.
